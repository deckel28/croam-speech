Open Speech Recording

We at Anjaneya Services, IIT Delhi are developing a product - CROAM which makes use of Keyword Spotting paradign of Artificial Intelligence. But there are very few open source collections of speech data where individual words are spoken by a large number of different people. This makes it tough to build good open source examples of detecting even very common spoken keywords such as "Yes", "No", "On", or "Off".
To train our model, we are trying to collect audio samples of words - 'HELP' and 'BACHAO' from 100s of people. We would be in prototype phase before the end of the summer and your few seconds of help would be wondorous to us.
To make sure that we're able to use your voice data for our Machine Learning model, we need to get your agreement formally. If you would like to participate in providing some samples, please review and confirm your agreement to the following:
